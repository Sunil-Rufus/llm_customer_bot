Service Description
AI will be a part of our lives. Learning how to properly use it will supercharge our students and prepare them for the future. In this four week four-session course, students will learn the power of AI and how to use it by creating a video. They will get to use the most cutting-edge tools such as ChatGPT and Runway ML. The learnings and habits formed in this course can benefit them for life. 

Courses are held once a week on the same day and same time. Students will be provided the know how to work on an assignment in between sessions to complete by the start of the next session.
